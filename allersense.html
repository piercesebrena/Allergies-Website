ALLERSENSE

Allersense is a real life allergy tracker that is for individuals who are looking to take control of their seasonal allergies and improve their quality of life.Users using this tracker are able to track their symptoms,wherever they live, on a daily basis.The user can implement details such as potential triggers, severity of the symptoms,what medications they used that day.Other users will be able to connect and share their symptoms as well in order to connect and find possible solutions.There is also doctor recommendations as well as over the counter medication recommendations as well.
